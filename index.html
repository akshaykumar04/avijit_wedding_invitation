Our Story...

Once in a while, right in the middle of an ordinary life, love gives us a fairy tale.


"Forever begins"


A journey of 1000 miles start with a single step and we were fortunate enough that our first step was the most memorable one. It all started on the eve of the birthday of chai lover. Coffee as you know can be bitter but yet addictive so is the nature of the coffee lover, the groom. Playing with words to give them a rhymic sequence is his hobby and thus what followed was a poem on her birthday.Though when we talk about the first step being the most memorable one, all credit goes to the families for arranging the first meet up, perks of arrange marriage setup. Tea and coffee both refresh you and comes in different form. It is thus imperative to get used to every taste that they offer. We started on that path through different questions and we must admit finding answers was not our goal as reaching the end of tea/coffee glass is the last thing you want. It was to discover eachother and know about the preferences. Tea and coffee owing to its addictive nature forms a bond that lasts for a lifetime, so is our love that will last till forever.It was an accidental coillison of meeting the right person at right place and at right time. As we know coffee can be instant but tea takes time to brew so is our relation, coffee lover loves to wait for tea lover. Love isn’t something you say, it’s something you do...
